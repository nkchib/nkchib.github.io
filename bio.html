About Me

Hi, my name is Nicholas Chib, and I am a student in the tenth grade at Ruben S. Ayala High School. One important thing about me is that I love music, especially Jazz. I have been playing the trombone since the fifth grade, as well as the euphonium since the sixth grade. I am currently a member of the Ayala Band and Colorguard, and Ayala's Jazz and Wind Ensembles. Along with my passion for music, I also am very interested in Engineering and Computer Science. From a young age, I have always wanted to know how and why things work. This inspired me to join Ayala's Engineering Acedemy. I am currently in my second year in the Engineering Academy, and I have already learned a lot about the engineering feild so far. As I continue to be a part of this unique program, my interest for the engineering feild grows, and I look forward to all of the things I have yet to learn.
